LinkedStack

A LinkedStack stores elements using a Last-In-First-Out (LIFO) policy as nodes connected by references.

The stack object keeps two fields: top and size. All insertions and removals happen at top.

Operations and asymptotic complexity

  • Push: adds a new node at the top. Complexity: O(1).
  • Pop: removes the node at the top. Complexity: O(1).
  • Top: reads the top element without removing it. Complexity: O(1).
